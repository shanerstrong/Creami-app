Quick Reference

ICE CREAM

Use for richer traditional bases

Higher fat, higher sugar, cream, whole milk, custard-style, or dense commercial-style recipes.

LITE ICE CREAM

Use for leaner or high-protein bases

Low-fat milk, protein powder, sugar substitutes, pudding mix, and reduced-calorie recipes.

RE-SPIN

Use after a powdery first spin

Re-spin once without liquid first. Add only a small amount of liquid if it stays dry.

FULL / TOP / BOTTOM

Match the button to the portion

Full processes the entire tub. Top and Bottom are for the corresponding section.

STOP

Do not process a raised frozen hump

Flatten the surface first. Stop if the machine is moving excessively, the pint is installed incorrectly, or the blade assembly is not secure.
